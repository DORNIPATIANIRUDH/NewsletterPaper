Grade 9 Science Fair: Three Vista teams heading to State competition

This Thursday's Science Fair saw 42 projects from Grades 8 and 9 compete for top honours. Judges from BITS Pilani commented that several projects were at undergraduate level. Three teams have been selected to represent Vista at the Telangana State Science Exhibition in July.

The winning project, by Sanya Nair (Grade 9), demonstrated a low-cost biochar water purification system. Second place went to a team developing an AI-powered early warning system for dengue outbreaks using satellite imagery.
