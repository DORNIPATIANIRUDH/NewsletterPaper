Our history

Vista through the years

2008
Vista International School founded
Opened with 120 students on a 4-acre campus in Gachibowli. First batch offered Cambridge IGCSE only.
2011
IB Diploma Programme launched
Became one of the first schools in Hyderabad to offer the full IB continuum from PYP to DP.
2015
Expanded to 1,000 students
New sports complex and performing arts centre opened. First alumni batch reached senior leadership roles.
2019
Alumni Network formally established
Vista Alumni Association launched with 800 members. First annual reunion held on campus.
2024
Ranked Top 5 International Schools in Hyderabad
Recognised by Education World rankings for academic excellence and holistic development.
2026
2,400+ alumni across 38 countries
Vista graduates now work at Google, Oxford, AIIMS, McKinsey, ISRO, and lead their own startups.
