From the principal's desk

A week of curiosity, courage & community

This week reminded us why Vista exists — not just to teach, but to inspire. From the Grade 9 science fair that drew gasps from every visitor, to the quiet moment a Grade 3 student helped a new classmate find their classroom, our school lived its values in beautiful, everyday ways.

As we approach the end of the academic year, I want to thank every parent, student, and teacher who has made 2025–26 unforgettable.

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

Student of the month

May 2026 spotlight

AR
Aarav Reddy
Grade 11 · IB DP
🥇 Athletics Gold House Captain CAS Leader

Aarav has been a standout student this year — not just for his gold medal in the 400m at Hyderabad Inter-School Athletics, but for the way he leads. As House Captain, he organised three community service drives this year alone.

His IB Extended Essay on "The Economics of Sports Infrastructure in Tier-2 Indian Cities" has been shortlisted for the regional IB Award for best EE.

"Vista taught me that being the best at one thing is never enough. You have to show up for others too."
